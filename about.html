THE SIGILS

6529 identity portrait · manifesto & anatomy

What is this?

Every 6529 wallet carries an identity — not chosen, but accrued. Cards held, cards made, memes rated, days on the manifold, reputation earned.

The Sigils reads nine of these signals and projects them as an orbital portrait. A sun, rings, beads, threads, thorns, rays. Each wallet renders differently, deterministically — your sigil is your own.

This is not an NFT. It is a mirror.

How it works

Data is pulled live from the 6529 API and refreshed every 10 minutes. As your holdings change — a new meme, a new rank, a new card — your sigil evolves.

The green dot in the corner signals live data. Click it to refresh manually. Orange means stale (API unreachable); click again to retry.

All visual parameters are derived. Nothing is random. Your wallet address seeds the axis of rotation, the orientation of rare forms, the phase of every shimmer — so no two sigils move alike.

Parameter anatomy

TDH amber sun at the center — your gravitational mass total days held across your consolidated wallets

REP sage rays converging inward from the void reputation given to you by other members — signals arriving
LEVEL coral progress arc — fills to level/100 collector level in the 6529 ecosystem, 0 to 100
FULL SET teal thorny outer shell — pulses every 2 seconds visible only if you hold all 484 Memes cards
NAKAMOTO gold bracelet on a tilted great circle — an orbiting ball travels it visible only if you hold The Memes Card #4 (Nakamoto)
MEME ARTIST rainbow ring with charms — one charm per card you made visible only if your handle appears as artist on a Memes card

Wallet consolidation is shown as small moons orbiting the sun — one extra moon per additional wallet in your consolidation.

Sigil Name

Every sigil carries a three-part name — a 6529 sigil name, shown under your tier.

The name is a modifier + an archetype + a suffix, chosen from your profile's dominant signals. It's deterministic: the same wallet always gets the same name, but with a dictionary of over fifty words per part, no two sigils read alike.

Examples: Golden Bearer of the Origin (Nakamoto holder) · Rising Maker of the Spark (early-tier artist) · Ancient Pillar of the Ledger (deep TDH, long-held) · Luminous Herald of the Archive (high REP) · Dormant Drifter of the Unseen (fresh wallet).

Modifiers include Golden/Gilded/Auric (Nakamoto), Blessed/Hallowed (Full Set), Ancient/Primordial/Fathomless (top tiers), Steadfast/Rooted/Anchored (Anchor tier), Rising/Ascending/Waxing (early growth), Luminous/Radiant/Incandescent (high REP), Amplified/Charged/Surging (boosted), Quiet/Silent/Hushed (introverted), Dormant/Slumbering/Latent (unborn), and Steady/Modest/Grounded (default).
Archetypes are drawn from your role: Maker/Author/Scribe/Crafter/Weaver (artists) · Bearer/Keystone/Titan/Warden (Nakamoto) · Cornerstone/Originator/Seed-Bearer (Nakamoto + artist) · Completist/Preserver/Whole-Keeper (full set) · Anchor/Pillar/Steward/Sentinel (TDH) · Curator/Archivist/Gatherer (unique) · Herald/Witness/Emissary/Speaker (REP) · Voice/Catalyst/Nexus/Conduit (NIC) · Adept/Sage/Elder/Master (level) · Drifter/Wanderer/Seeker/Pilgrim (balanced).
Suffixes root the name in place: of the Origin (Nakamoto), of the 484 (full set), of the Hand (artist), of the Unseen (unborn), or one of fifteen general lines — of the Archive, of the Long Day, of the Horizon, of the Spiral, and others.

Reading your sigil

Hover any ring (or tap on mobile) to see what it is, what value it carries, and what it means.

The tier name, memetic score, and TDH appear top-right. The nine base parameters are listed bottom-left; the ring color legend bottom-right.

Scroll with your mouse or drag the side slider to zoom. Axis of rotation is seeded by your wallet — no two sigils tilt alike.

FAQ

Does my sigil change over time?
Yes. As your TDH, REP, holdings, or artist credits change, your sigil evolves with the next refresh.

I'm a Memes artist but no rainbow ring shows up?
Your handle in the 6529 profile may differ from the one credited on the card. Wallet-based matching usually catches this; if not, data may be stale in the public API.

Can I export my sigil?
Yes — PNG (still), GIF (12s loop, Twitter-safe), or MP4 (15s, full quality). Use the EXPORT button.

Is this an NFT or collection?
Not today. It's a free tool for the community, a mirror. What comes next is a conversation for the community to have.
